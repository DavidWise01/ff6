## The four natures of emergence

FFVI's central war — magic/Espers vs. Magitek/machines — runs right across the taxonomy:

- 🟢 **natural** — born of flesh and the living world *(Sabin, Cyan, Locke, Gau, Setzer, Shadow…)*
- 🟣 **ethereal** — magic, the Esper, the painted-alive, the enigma *(Terra, Relm, Strago, Gogo)*
- 🟡 **spiritual** — the soul and the calling — or, darkly, ascension to godhood *(Kefka)*
- 🔵 **electrical** — Magitek, the machinist, magic from technology *(Celes, Edgar)*
